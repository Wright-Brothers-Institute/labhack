Welcome to the next level

The Swarm and Search AI Competition Series is excited to announce the 2021 Challenge: Capture the Flag. This year's virtual competition will build on the 2019 Swarm and Search AI Challenge: Fire Hack, but will push the AI much further than before. Participant teams will not only compete against each other, but against agents designed to push their algorithms to the limit. A collaborative exchange formulated by the Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL) in the United States and the Defence Science and Technology Laboratory (DSTL) in the UK, will bring together competitive AI experts from around the world. Can your skills capture the flag and conquer the competition?

Registration is scheduled to begin in Feb 2021, with the virtual competition starting in Mar 2021 and running through to a final around May 2021.
