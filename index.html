Join us for LabHack, a 26-hour hackathon brought to you by Code for America through support of the Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL) and Wright Brothers Institute. This is a chance to show off your programing, design and data science skills by building solutions to real-world challenges that AFRL researchers encounter every day. We provide the food, location, big network pipelines and challenges. There's cash prizes for the top three teams. We also have workshops, talks, swag and fun events along the way. Best of all, this event is 100% free and open to the public.
  • What: A 26-hour hackathon around sensor data
  • When: Saturday, 25 October 2014 - Sunday, 26 October 2014
  • Where: WBI Tec^Edge Innovation and Collaboration Center
  • Why: To learn something new, build something awesome and have fun!
  • Who: This event is open to the public. Anyone, military or civilian, may participate.
For more questions and answers, see our FAQ.
